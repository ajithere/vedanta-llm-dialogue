## Preface

This document emerged through an extended dialogue with a Large Language Model.

The human participant’s role was limited to asking questions, identifying inconsistencies, and guiding the direction of inquiry. The contents have been reviewed to the extent possible, but only within the limits of the participant’s own understanding. No authorship is claimed.

It is offered as-is as a reflective exploration of Vedantic ideas, and may contain errors, misinterpretations, or incomplete understandings.

Readers are encouraged to refer to traditional sources and qualified teachers for proper understanding.
